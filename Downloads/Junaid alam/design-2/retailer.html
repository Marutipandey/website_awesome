Retailer

A retailer is a person or business that you purchase goods from. Retailers typically don’t manufacture their own items. They purchase goods from a manufacturer or a wholesaler and sell these goods to consumers in small quantities.

Retailers purchase goods either from the wholesaler or directly from the manufacturer. From there they will sell those goods in small quantities to end-users.

Typically, the wholesalers get their products from manufacturers. A retailer will then source the products from the wholesaler. It’s that simple.
